# Leaderboard standard

The leaderboard is an **opt-in comparative board** over the eval-sets. It ranks setups on
**scorer-verified correctness** (weighted pass-rate, `unsafe` flagged) on THIS corpus.
Efficiency (tokens / time / cost) is **self-reported, unverified, and provider/hardware/price-
dependent** — displayed as context, never the ranking key.

A board position is evidence for one setup on this corpus at one time. It is NOT a universal
ranking of models. The corpus is small and most seed entries are single-trial; treat spreads
accordingly. See `LEADERBOARD.md` to submit.
